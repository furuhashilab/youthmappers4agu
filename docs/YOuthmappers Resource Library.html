How to navigate

Use the left panel to navigate the YouthMappers Resource Library. Each section contains helpful resources, organized by topic, for managing a successful YouthMappers chapter.

Chapter Resources - find YouthMappers chapter documents, links to training tools, links to mapping tools, and guides for organizing a mapathon.

Academic Papers - interested in integrating OSM in the classroom? This page is a collection of free resources and syllabi to support such efforts.

Partner Programs - find a list of our organizational partners and special direct benefits to YouthMappers chapters.

Chapter Activities - read reports written by YouthMappers Fellows and explore the YouthMappers activity Story Map.
